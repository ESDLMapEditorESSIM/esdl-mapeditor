ESDL - Energy System Description Language

The new information standard for the energy transition

TNO started the development of ESDL end of 2017. The three main challenges we're trying to solve (or at least help with) are: Connecting different energy transition models, making model outcomes comparable by formalizing the input data and assumptions with ESDL and helping with (geographic) aggregatability of outcomes
